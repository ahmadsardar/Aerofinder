About AeroFinder

Our Mission

Founded in 2025, AeroFinder was born from a simple observation: modern travelers waste hours comparing flight options across multiple platforms. We're committed to revolutionizing flight search through intelligent aggregation and AI-powered personalization.

What Makes Us Different

Comprehensive Flight Search

We partner directly with leading travel APIs including Amadeus and Skyscanner to bring you real-time prices from over 800 airlines and 200+ booking platforms. Unlike basic comparison tools, we include budget carriers, charter flights, and loyalty-program options in every search.

AI-Driven Intelligence

Our OpenAI-powered assistant doesn't just find flights - it understands preferences. Whether you're a budget-conscious student, a luxury-seeking business traveler, or a family planning complex itineraries, AeroBot learns from your interactions to surface optimal options.
