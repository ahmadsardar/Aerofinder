Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using AeroFinder for your flight bookings.

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a flight?

To book a flight:

  1. Enter your departure and arrival cities
  2. Select your travel dates
  3. Choose the number of passengers and cabin class
  4. Click "Search Flights"
  5. Select your preferred flight from the results
  6. Follow the booking process to complete your reservation
Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking through your account dashboard. Please note that modification and cancellation policies vary by airline and fare type. Some bookings may be non-refundable or subject to fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and various local payment methods depending on your region.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

To create an account:

  1. Click the "Login" button in the top right corner
  2. Select "Create Account"
  3. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile information
How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click the "Login" button
  2. Select "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Follow the instructions in the email you receive

Flight Information

How do I check my flight status?

You can check your flight status in several ways:

  • Through your account dashboard
  • Using the flight number on our homepage
  • By contacting the airline directly
What is the baggage allowance?

Baggage allowances vary by airline and fare type. You can find specific baggage information when booking your flight or by checking the airline's website. Generally, economy class includes one checked bag, while business and first class may include more.

Technical Support

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience technical issues:

  1. Try refreshing the page
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try using a different browser
  4. Contact our support team if the issue persists
Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information. Our security measures include:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security audits
  • Compliance with data protection regulations
