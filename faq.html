Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does AeroFinder get flight data?

We use APIs like Amadeus and Skyscanner to ensure real-time, accurate flight information.

2. Are the chatbot recommendations reliable?

Yes! Our OpenAI-powered chatbot analyzes your preferences to suggest tailored destinations.

3. Can I filter by airline?

Absolutely. Use the "Filter by Airline" dropdown on search results.

4. Is my payment information safe?

We don't store payment details. All transactions are handled by secure third-party providers.

5. How do I contact support?

Email us at support@aerofinder.com or use the chatbot for quick queries.
