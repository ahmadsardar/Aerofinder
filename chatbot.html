Baggage Policies

Most airlines allow 1 cabin bag (max 7kg) and 1 checked bag (typically 23kg) for international flights. Budget airlines may charge extra. Always check with your specific airline for exact limits.

Visa Requirements

Visa requirements vary by destination and nationality. For US citizens, popular destinations like Europe (ETIAS coming 2025), UK, and Japan typically allow visa-free stays for tourism. Always verify with official government sources.
